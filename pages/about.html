My Journey

I'm currently pursuing BE Mechanical Engineering and MSc Mathematics at BITS Pilani, one of India's premier engineering institutions. My academic journey has been marked by a passion for combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on practical experience.

As an NTSE (National Talent Search Examination) Scholar, I've demonstrated excellence not just in academics, but also in problem-solving and analytical thinking. This recognition has fueled my drive to push boundaries and explore innovative solutions in mechanical engineering.

What Drives Me

I'm fascinated by the intersection of traditional mechanical engineering and cutting-edge technology. From working with Building Information Modeling (BIM) systems to exploring swarm-based UAV technologies, I constantly seek opportunities to apply engineering principles to solve real-world challenges.

My experience spans across multiple domains:

  • Aerospace Technology: Gained hands-on experience at Khageshvara Aviation Technology Pvt Ltd, working on aviation systems
  • Research & Innovation: Developed swarm-based UAV systems for object tracking, combining robotics with intelligent algorithms
  • Computational Analysis: Certified in Computational Fluid Mechanics, applying CFD principles to engineering problems
